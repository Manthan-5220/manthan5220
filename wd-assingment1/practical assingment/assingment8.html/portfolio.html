    2. Cricket is a bat-and-ball sport played between two teams of eleven players each. Originating in England, it has grown to become one of the most popular sports in countries like India, Australia, England, Pakistan, and South Africa. The game is played on a circular or oval-shaped field with a 22-yard pitch at the center. There are several formats of cricket, including Test matches (lasting up to five days), One Day Internationals (ODIs), and Twenty20 (T20) matches, which are shorter and more fast-paced. The objective is for one team to score more runs than the other, with teams taking turns to bat and bowl
